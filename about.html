OUR NARRATIVE

Company Story

Moodboard Media is a creative ecosystem built around storytelling, visual media, and industry empowerment. Founded with the belief that every brand and filmmaker has a unique story to tell, we bring together creative production, film education, and story development under one roof.

What started as a small collective of filmmakers has grown into a platform that supports both commercial brands and independent storytellers. We don't just produce content — we cultivate ideas, nurture voices, and build bridges between emerging talent and the industry.

Today, Moodboard Media operates through three core pillars: a full-service creative agency, an initiative dedicated to women in film, and a personal consultancy for narrative development. Each arm shares the same DNA: cinematic quality, human connection, and the power of visual language.

FOUNDER & CREATIVE PRODUCER

Lesego Soke

Lesego Soke is a South African filmmaker, creative producer, and story strategist with over a decade of experience across theatre, television, and film.

Her journey began in theatre, with her debut production staged at Johannesburg's Joburg Theatre Fringe Festival before traveling to the National Arts Festival in Grahamstown.

She later trained in documentary filmmaking and went on to work in advertising and corporate television before spending seven years in long-form television as a script supervisor.

Today she works as a creative producer and consultant, developing projects that sit at the intersection of storytelling, culture, and strategy.
