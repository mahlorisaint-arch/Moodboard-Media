OUR NARRATIVE

Company Story

Moodboard Media is a creative ecosystem built around storytelling, visual media, and industry empowerment.

What started as a small collective of filmmakers has grown into a platform that supports both commercial brands and independent storytellers.

Today, Moodboard Media operates through three core pillars: a full-service creative agency, an initiative dedicated to women in film, and a personal consultancy for narrative development.

FOUNDER & CREATIVE PRODUCER

Lesego Soke

Lesego Soke is a South African filmmaker, creative producer, and story strategist with over a decade of experience.

Today she works as a creative producer and consultant, developing projects that sit at the intersection of storytelling, culture, and strategy.
