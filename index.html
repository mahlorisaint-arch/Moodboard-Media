Female Film Trailblazers

Female Film Trailblazers is an initiative founded by filmmaker and creative producer Lesego Soke. The platform was created to bridge the gap in representation, industry knowledge, compliance and community within the film and television industry for women.

Through curated conversations, workshops, and industry events, the initiative creates a space where experienced professionals can share knowledge with emerging filmmakers while building a supportive community of women shaping the future of African storytelling.

Impact in Action

Trailblazers 2024

Gatekeeping: opening conversations around access and opportunity.

Trailblazers 2024 – “Gatekeeping”

The 2024 edition focused on opening conversations around access to knowledge and opportunities within the film and television industry. The event brought together experienced professionals and emerging creatives for a day of meaningful conversation, networking, podcast interviews, photography sessions and a light brunch.

2025 Vision: empower 100 young women

The 2025 vision aims to empower 100 young women from township communities, helping them transform their creative talents into sustainable business opportunities within the film industry. The initiative will focus on improving creative literacy, industry knowledge, and understanding of the compliance and administrative structures required to build sustainable creative careers.

Topics for 2025 Summit

  • Compliance requirements for broadcasters
  • Digital marketing tools for creatives
  • Film festival financing and entry requirements
  • Professional compliance for actors
  • Diversifying income streams in the film industry

2025 Event Format

Workshop in Soweto (100 pax) Digital magazine feature Podcast roundtable Networking brunch

Lesego Says
